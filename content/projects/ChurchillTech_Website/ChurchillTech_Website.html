About This Website

This website was designed specifically according to the nord color scheme, as a custom template for Preston Pan's static site generator.

Minimal

This website was designed to be as minimal as possible to increase the amount of people who can read and spread the word of free software. We wanted to make our own website so that we could say what we have to say freely without the need for large firms dictating discourse.

On slow internet connections, and even on fast ones, you will notice that this website loads faster than most others due to its minimal nature.
